All datafiles are available in csv format, with the macro panel also available as an rds file. 

The rest of the material are codebooks and data descriptions minus the autocratizing episodes measures from Pelke and Croissant (2021), available in their article: Pelke, L. and Croissant, A. (2021), Conceptualizing and Measuring Autocratization Episodes. Swiss Polit Sci Rev, 27: 434-448.
